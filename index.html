Writing

Curb Magazine

Curb is a magazine produced by 21 students in UW-Madison's School of Journalism and Mass Communication. I served as editor of the 2014 edition, where I finalized the editorial philosophy and facilitated story assignments and development. I also wrote a feature piece and conducted a Q&A with Chef Tory Miller.

The Daily Cardinal

The Daily Cardinal is UW-Madison's only daily newspaper. I wrote for the arts section for two semesters.

WSUM

In addition to serving as WSUM 91.7 FM's Program Director, Music Director and a Traffic Director, I also contributed to the website. I gathered our DJs' favorite albums of 2013 when I was Music Director and conducted an interview with Saint Pepsi as promo for WSUM's annual music festival in 2014.
